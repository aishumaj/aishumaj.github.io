About Me

I have spent the last near-decade working in the healthcare industry prior to deciding to transition careers and learn to code. I started exploring code by self-teaching Javascript over a month, before joining a full-stack web development bootcamp with Rithm School, which I am currently enrolled in.

Prior to my decision to pivot careers, I graduated with a degree in Molecular Cell Biology from The University of California, Berkeley, followed by two years as a medical assistant with Planned Parenthood, and some time enrolled as a medical student. While I no longer work on the patient-facing side of healthcare, I remain passionate about patient and healthcare worker advocacy, and I hope to be able to work on technology that can aid providers in increasing efficiency and ease of their work and improve quality of care.

Take a look at my resume for more information about my past professional experiences, or shoot me a message to say hi!
